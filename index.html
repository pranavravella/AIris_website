Atheia

Innovative Features

Facial Recognition

Recognizes faces of people you interact with everyday.

Text Reader

Scan text from anywhere and have it read to you.

Search Mode

Actively search for objects in your vicinity and know how far they are.

Real-Time

Results are produced within seconds through our onboard processing.

Real-Time Stereo Processing

Our device can process a stereo live stream in real-time allowing us to actively locate objects in 3d space. We do this by creating a depth map, which can retrieve the distances to objects in the picture, which is key to our search mode.
